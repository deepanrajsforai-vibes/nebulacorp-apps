Privacy Policy

TV Remote for Android TV · Last updated: 9 June 2026

Short version: this app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It talks only to your TV on your local Wi-Fi network.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, usage data, or device identifiers.

What stays on your device

To pair with and reconnect to your TV, the app stores a security key and the TV's identity in your device's secure keychain. This information never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app or unpair the TV.

Network access

The app communicates only with your Android TV / Google TV device over your local Wi-Fi network, using the TV's standard pairing protocol. It does not send any information to us or to any external server. It makes no internet requests.

Local Network permission

iOS asks for Local Network access so the app can discover and connect to your TV on Wi-Fi. This permission is used solely for that purpose. If you decline it, the app cannot find your TV but collects nothing either way.

Children's privacy

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
