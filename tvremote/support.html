Getting started

  1. Make sure your iPhone and your Android TV are on the same Wi-Fi network.
  2. Open the app — it searches for your TV automatically.
  3. Tap your TV when it appears, then enter the PIN shown on the TV screen to pair.
  4. That's it — pair once and you're connected.

Frequently asked

The app can't find my TV.
Confirm both devices are on the same Wi-Fi (not a guest network), the TV is powered on, and you allowed Local Network access when prompted. You can also tap Connect by IP address and enter the TV's IP manually.

It says "Couldn't reach your TV."
The TV may be asleep or off Wi-Fi. Make sure it's on, then tap Retry. If the TV was restarted or factory reset, use Pair Again to re-pair securely.

Do I need an account?
No. There are no accounts, no sign-up, and no ads.

Is my data collected?
No. The app talks only to your TV on your local network. See our Privacy Policy.

Which TVs are supported?
Android TV and Google TV devices that support the standard remote pairing protocol (most modern Android TV boxes, sticks, and built-in TVs).
