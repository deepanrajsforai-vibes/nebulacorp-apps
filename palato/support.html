Common questions

Do I need an account?

No. Palato works fully offline. An account is optional and only adds cloud backup and family sharing.

How does family sharing work?

Sign in, then share your 6-character invite code with a family member. When they enter it, you both see and edit the same recipe library.

Where are my recipes stored?

On your device by default. If you sign in, they're also backed up to our server so they sync across your devices. See our Privacy Policy.

How do the AI features work?

"Ask the AI Chef" and AI text-import send the text you provide to Google's Gemini to generate a recipe. These features are optional.

How do I delete my account?

Email us and we'll remove your account and any backed-up data from our server. Signing out alone keeps everything on your device.
