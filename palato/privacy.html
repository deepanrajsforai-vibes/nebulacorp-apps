Privacy Policy

Palato · NebulCorp · Last updated: 14 June 2026

Short version: Palato works fully offline. You only share data with us if you choose to create an account to back up and sync your recipes. We do not run ads, analytics, or third-party trackers, and we never sell your data.

What stays on your device

By default, everything — your recipes, photos, meal plans, shopping lists, and the on-device "taste" preferences Palato learns from how you cook — lives only on your iPhone. Voice commands in Cook Mode are processed on-device and are never recorded or sent anywhere.

What we collect (only if you create an account)

Creating a free account is optional and exists solely to back up your library and share it with family. If you do, we store:

  • Your email address — to identify your account. If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive only what Apple provides (a stable ID and, if you allow it, your email).
  • Your recipe content — recipes, photos, plans and lists you choose to back up, so they sync across your devices and to family members you invite.

This data is stored on our backend (Convex) and is linked to your account. It is used only to provide sync and family sharing — never for advertising or profiling.

AI features

When you use an AI feature (e.g. "Ask the AI Chef" or importing a recipe from pasted text), the text you provide is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. That request is subject to Google's API data-use terms. We do not send your account identity with these requests, and AI features are optional.

What we do NOT collect

No location, no contacts, no advertising identifiers, no usage analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs of any kind.

Deleting your data

You can sign out at any time, which keeps your recipes on-device and stops syncing. To delete your account and the data backed up to our server, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

Children

Palato is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
